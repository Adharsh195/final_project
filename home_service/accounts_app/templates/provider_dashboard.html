About Us

Empowering local professionals to grow their business

Welcome to HomeCare, a trusted home service marketplace designed to help skilled service providers connect with customers, manage bookings easily, and grow their business with confidence.

Why We Exist

We understand the challenges service professionals face—finding regular work, managing customers, and receiving timely payments. Our platform simplifies everything by bringing genuine customers directly to you.

What We Offer Providers

  • Increased visibility to nearby customers
  • Easy booking and schedule management
  • Secure and transparent payments
  • Ratings and reviews to build trust
  • Fair opportunities for all professionals

Our Mission

Our mission is to support local service providers by offering digital tools that increase income, simplify work, and help build long-term customer relationships.

Who Can Join?

Electricians, plumbers, cleaners, AC technicians, painters, carpenters, appliance repair professionals, and more. If you deliver quality service, this platform is for you.
